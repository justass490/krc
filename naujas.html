We are Sofa

Sofa is a software and interaction design company. We develop products and help others design theirs.

Products

Effective software starts with a good user experience. We strive to delight people with beautiful UI and solid interaction models in our products.

Work

Besides creating our own products, we help others to design and refire theirs. From mockup or prototype to complete products. Or anything in between.

Company

Sofa was founded late 2006 and is based in Amsterdam, the Netherlands. We are a small privately held company that specializes in interaction design.

Blog
